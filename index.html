Jiye Kim

Ph.D. Candidate  ·  Seoul National University

I am a Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at Seoul National University, advised by Prof. Jongho Lee.

My research focuses on quantitative MRI, particularly Quantitative Susceptibility Mapping (QSM) and χ-separation, combined with deep learning for high-resolution in-vivo brain imaging. I am broadly interested in MRI physics, signal processing, and RF pulse design.
